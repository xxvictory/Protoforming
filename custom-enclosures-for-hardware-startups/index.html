Enclosure details that affect production

A good enclosure quote needs more than outer dimensions. The internal structure, fastening method, sealing target, heat, finish, and assembly plan all affect cost and manufacturability.

Structure and assembly

Review ribs, bosses, snap fits, screws, inserts, PCB supports, cable exits, and parting lines.

Prototype route

Choose CNC, 3D printing, sheet metal, or tooling based on quantity, function, finish, and timeline.

Sealing and finish

Plan O-rings, gaskets, texture, paint, anodizing, silk print, and inspection points before production.

Useful enclosure RFQ inputs

Send enough context for us to understand both the part and the product it protects.

  • 3D CAD, 2D drawing, or photos of the product concept
  • PCB or internal component envelope if relevant
  • Material, finish, color, texture, and logo requirements
  • Waterproof, impact, heat, UV, or outdoor requirements
  • Prototype quantity, pilot batch size, and launch timeline
