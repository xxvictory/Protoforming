Key RFQ details for injection molding

Better inputs help avoid quotes that look cheap but miss tooling or production risks.

  • 3D files, 2D drawings, and assembly relationship
  • Prototype quantity, first batch quantity, and annual estimate
  • Material, color, surface finish, and visible surfaces
  • Critical dimensions, sealing, clips, threads, or inserts
  • Target timeline for T1 sample and first shipment
